Milestones
3/2023

ComfySSH - Ideation

Frustrated by the high costs and technical headaches of screens in his cyberdeck builds, Thomas created ComfySSH. This innovative application makes cyberdeck projects cheaper, easier, more efficient, and higher quality.

5/2023

Comfy - Pivot

Having achieved his original goal, Thomas developed a highly optimized wireless communication algorithm for robotics. Now, he aims to simplify all robotics projects with his new application, Comfy.

7/2023

Comfy v0.1

Comfy v0.1 is complete, enabling remote control of a small RC car.

8/2023

Jax the robot

Comfy v0.2 is complete, along with a more advanced RC car named Jax. Jax features powerful wheels and a top-mounted camera.

9/2023

Feedback from a passionate user

Thomas's YouTube video about robotics and AI went viral. One viewer, Harooq, enthusiastically used the application and provided valuable feedback on how to improve its design for a modern audience.

10/2023 - 3/2024

Deep hibernation

During this time, Thomas spent nights and weekends rebuilding the application for a better user experience. He dedicated significant effort to developing the website, creating YouTube content, and researching the makers and creators market to ensure Comfy was a worthwhile pursuit. During this period, Thomas and Harooq also became close friends.

3/2024 - 5/2024

ComfySpace was born

ComfySpace launched with a revamped, more robust application that's appealing to makers. It also features a website with valuable robotics lessons and project documentation.

6/2024

Buildspace s5

Thomas was accepted into Buildspace, a program designed to help people develop their ideas more effectively. During this time, Comfyspace experienced significant growth.

Comfy FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What is Comfy Space?
Comfy Space is a cross-platform application & your personal gateway to the world of personal robotics, regardless of your skill level. From learning with Comfy Academy to creating with Comfy Projects, we support your journey from concept to creation. Just share your idea, and our team will guide you through the rest!
What is the advantage of using Comfy Space?
Comfy Space is a refreshing change in the robotics development process. Our emphasis is on nurturing your creativity and helping you bring your unique vision to life. With Comfy Space, the journey through robotics becomes less of a challenge and more of a delightful and rewarding experience.
What is Comfy Academy?
Comfy Academy is a part of Comfy Space which offers lessons and projects to help users understand robotics. These lessons are designed to be approachable to every experience level. Jump in & you would find something interesting!
How can I download Comfy Space?
Comfy Space can be downloaded from the App Store and Google Play. Soon, you will also be able to access Comfy Space on the web!
